 Goals and partnersWill LaBar, Carlee Alm-LaBar
  • Carlee: Smart City Award has catalyzed smart city initiatives in LFT
  • Goals:
    • Deploy 300 sensors
    • Community involvement in purchasing and using sensors
    • ID partners and project sustainability
    • Be transparent: make data open and describing data management plans
  • Objectives and expected benefits
    • ID best practices for managing big data within 9 parishes in Acadiana
    • Learn how LFT will manage large volumes of Smart City data
    • Learn how to increase civic engagement in smart city, info tech. and the environment
    • How to use data from many sensors to understand environmental conditions and its relationship to human health
    • Provide other cities, businesses, citizens, and EPA with real-world lessons re. data mgmt
  • Collaborative
    • LCG, UL, CGI major players
    • LPSS, LEDA, OneAcadiana, SLCC, Downtown Lafayette, LPS
    • LUS Fiber
    • LCG:
      • Sensor siting facilities (traffic poles)
      • Experience with IoT for traffic congestion mgmt
    • UL:
      • Use community at a living laboratory
      • Goal: Raise awareness of env. quality, including air quality
      • Chem. dept. help with placing sensors
      • Geosci. dept. use data in students' GIS projects
      • CVDI big data and visualization and analytics

        • How do you handle data from IoT
