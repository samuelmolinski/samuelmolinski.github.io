Goals

  • Provide citizens and interested parties with a notification panel, so they can follow the "environmental heartbeat" of the city as well as their own impacts and contributions.

  • Populate the profile picture with third party profile pairing (i.e. FB, Twitter, or Google+ profile picture)

  • Notifications to be handled through social media or email
  • Populate notifications with 'green points'
  • Possible partnership with Project Front Yard.

Background and strategic fit

Assumptions

  • User has an email address, Facebook, Twitter, or Google+ account.
